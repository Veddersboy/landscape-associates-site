About Landscape Associates

Landscape Associates is a design-build-maintain studio focused on residential and small commercial gardens. We like soft edges, honest materials, and spaces that feel quietly looked after.

Most of our work comes from repeat clients, neighbors, and people who’ve seen our gardens in person.
